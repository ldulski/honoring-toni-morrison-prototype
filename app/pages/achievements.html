Impact on Civil Rights

Morrison’s work carries a heavy load, giving historical accuracy and focusing on the people left out of history. Her novels tell the stories of lives led by African-American people for centuries. Discussing African-American people and culture influenced hundreds of authors that came after her to share their stories.

Her legacy

Toni Morrison had an unwavering commitment to social justice and upheld the belief that the responsibility to make the world a better place lies in our hands. By reading stories by Black authors, you are uplifting Black voices and educating yourself about the Black community.
